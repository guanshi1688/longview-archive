# Medium Draft Index

This folder stores external English essays prepared for Medium publication.

These drafts are part of the external publication layer of Longview Archive｜观势档案.  
They are not the master outline, and they do not expose the full theoretical structure.

The purpose of this folder is to preserve early English drafts, organize publication batches, track publishing status, and provide a reference point for future Medium / Substack releases.

---

## Archive Principle

The essays in this folder are external notes.

They should be written as independent, third-party-style essays rather than as direct chapters of the Longview master framework.

Each essay should:

- focus on one concept or one case;
- avoid exposing the full theoretical map;
- create an English search anchor;
- remain readable as a standalone Medium article;
- preserve the formation history of the external English layer.

The master outline is not stored here.

## Medium Formatting Rule

Medium is a rich-text editor, not a Markdown-first publishing system.

When preparing the final version, each essay should be copied in this structure:

Title

Subtitle

Body text

Topics

Recommended topics may include:

- History
- China
- Political Economy
- Development
- Global South
- Industrialization
- Infrastructure
- Civilization
- Economics
- Geopolitics

Do not use heavy Markdown syntax in the final Medium copy.

---

## Writing Style

Medium essays should be:

- clear;
- calm;
- analytical;
- third-party in tone;
- readable without prior knowledge of Longview Archive;
- focused on one concept or case;
- free of direct exposure of the master outline.

Avoid phrases such as:

- “This is the full theory.”
- “The master framework is...”
- “The complete structure is...”
- “This proves the whole system...”

Prefer phrases such as:

- “One way to understand this problem is...”
- “This note uses the term...”
- “A useful distinction is...”
- “The issue is not simply X, but Y.”
- “This is less a question of policy than of structure.”
