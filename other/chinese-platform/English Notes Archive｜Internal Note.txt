# English Notes Archive｜Internal Note

This directory contains the English notes archive of Longview Archive｜观势档案.

These texts are not platform drafts in the ordinary sense. They are archive versions of short English essays, conceptual notes, and historical reflections related to production systems, institutional absorption, value capture, civilizational form, and long-term social change.

Platform-published versions may appear on Medium, Substack, or other public channels under the name Evan Vale. Those versions may contain minor formatting differences, platform-specific headings, images, subtitles, or links.

Unless otherwise stated, the archive version in this repository should be treated as the reference version.

## Current Series

### The Architecture of Production and Capture

This series forms the main theoretical layer of the English notes archive.

It examines production systems, absorptive capacity, external capital, state function, social reproduction, value capture, and the structural limits of development.

### Frontiers

This series forms the historical case layer of the English notes archive.

It examines civilizational expansion, frontier absorption, state capacity, maritime limits, institutional replication, and the structural boundary of expansion.

## Publication Principle

The public-facing versions of these essays should remain restrained.

They should avoid promotional language, personal appeals, exaggerated claims, aggressive branding, or unnecessary explanations of the broader project.

The purpose of external publication is not immediate traffic acquisition.

It is concept deposition, search presence, archive formation, and long-term recognition of a coherent theoretical structure.

## Citation and Attribution

These texts are original works of Longview Archive｜观势档案.

Short quotation, discussion, and citation are acceptable with proper attribution to the author or archive source.

Unauthorized reproduction, rewriting, translation, commercial use, bulk extraction, or republication is not permitted.

## Internal Position

The English notes archive is not the master outline of Longview Archive.

It is an external-facing conceptual layer.

Its purpose is to make selected terms, arguments, and structural explanations available in English without exposing the full Chinese master framework.
